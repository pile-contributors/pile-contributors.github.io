CMake

Piles use CMake as a build system. Several CMake scripts are provided in pile-cmake repository to make the job of the developer as simple as possible. Here is a complete list:

  • pileCmakeOutputPath
  • pileRelativePath
  • pileUsedBy
  • pileDebugMessage
  • pileInclude
  • pileCreateCopyTarget
  • pileCopyHeaders
  • pileSetDependencies
  • pileSetCategory
  • pileSetTags
  • pileSetVersion
  • pileSetMode
  • pileSetCommon
  • pileConfigFile
  • pileSetSources
